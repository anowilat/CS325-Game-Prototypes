Killer for Hire

  • High Concept:
    Players take on the role of a contracted assassin sent to murder a patient recovering from an organ transplant.
    To get to the patient, the assassin must navigate through guards patroling the hospital halls and might run into
    a few obstacles along the way.

  • Theme:
    • Revenge
    • Organ implants

  • Mandated Variety:
    • Input: keyboard
    • Randomness: deterministic
    • Play Styles: explorer
    • Genres: adventure, stealth

  • Player Experience Goals:
    • Objectives; the game tries something in the way of requiring players to meet objectives before progressing further.
    • Stealth and suspense; the player must navigate through a few guards and avoid being caught in order to reach their
      ultimate objective and assassinate the patient.
    • Slight world exploration; the players are given free roam to the (very small) environment where the game takes place;
      giving them freedom to play it how they deem fit.

  • Gameplay:
    Players navigate the assassin using keyboard arrows. They must get to the patients room, but run might need to collect
    a key from another room before-hand as a side objective. Along the way, the must avoid the patroling guards and their
    flashlights; keeping in mind that the guards also have a small periphary surrounding them.
    IMPORTANT NOTE: The games gameplay is not complete:
    • Two of the guards have a bug that prevents them from moving and they end up blocking the way.
    • The walls should not be passable. I started the prototype using the P2 physics engine which worked
      better with transparent PNG spaces, but ultimately had to change the entire prototype to ARCADE
      because of difficulties encountered. There was also not enough time to individually add squares
      or rectangles as walls individually.

  • Prototype Goal:
    What I hoped to accomplish/practice in this prototype are the following:
    • Setting design and interaction with the enviroment.
    • Character movements and animations with those movements.
    • Requiring objectives in order to progress to another part of the game.
    • Further practice with the Physics systems: ARCADE and P2JS

  • Features:
    The game employs stealth like mechanisms which could be developed for additional levels had their been enough time.
    For the prototype, there is not much action for the player to do except to evade guards and move their character
    around until they get to the desired destination.

  • Setting/Premise:
    The setting of the game is at a hospital, presumably during late hours in the recovery space.

  • Story:
    The main character is a contracted assassin sent to murder a unidentified patient. The motives of the assassination
    are unclear and neither are they of importance to the assassin; presumably spite, revenge, or the like.

  • Target Audience:
    Although the prototype is short and there is little reference to violence except for the end, a fuller version of the
    game might contain more violence and would therefore be unsuitable for children. However it can be targeted for anyone
    interested in the stealth genre.

  • Play Time:
    The game can take somewhere between 5 - 10 minutes.

  • Strategies:
    A good strategy is to keep a fair distance from the guards, as they have unmarked peripheral sense and will apprehend
    the player if they are near enough.
    It might also be advisable to find the key to the med room early so as to avoid the guards.
