Crash

  • High Concept:
    Players race against two computer-controlled cars, however they must abide by the rules of the road;
    making sure to observe the stoplight or risk crashing into another car.

  • Theme:
    • Car accident

  • Mandated Variety:
    • Input: Clickable Buttons
    • Randomness: Some Randomness
    • Play styles: --
    • Genres: Racer

  • Player Experience Goals:
    • Experience a 2D fast-paced racer; where players control their own vehicles.
    • Suspense and risk; players must make quick decisions on how to drive and whether they risk themselves.
    • Scoreboarding and oppurtunity to score better on subsequent trials.

  • Gameplay:
    Players race against two computer-controlled cars and must survive a chosen number of laps by following the
    rules or risking themselves. As players lap through, a stoplight will call a specific signal color: green, yellow,
    red at each lap. The breakdown of the signals is as follows:
    • Green: Players may pass without fear of crashing.
    • Red: Players must stop or they will crash.
    • Yellow: Players have the option to pass onto the next block or stop, but there is a 50/50 chance of crashing.

  • Prototype Goal:
    • To iterate and further polish a designed prototype from physical to digital

  • Features:
    All efforts in this iteration was spent polishing the aesthetics, cleaning up bugs, and adding new features.
    See "Prototype Closest Other Game" for further details.

  • Setting/Premise:
    The setting is suburban for this prototype.
    The initial premise for the game was that of a street, rather than professional, type race.

  • Story:
    Prototype has no story other than the race itself.

  • Target Audience:
    This game is suitable for all ages, although some might feel it needs parental guidance regarding the car crashes.
    Futhermore, this game might be interesting for gamers who enjoy racer games.

  • Play Time:
    A single race often takes upwards of 5 minutes, but can be shorter or longer than that. Lap options range from
    5 to endless mode; therefore the game can take as little as 1 minute to infinitely long.

  • Strategies:
    Opponent cars start immediately and there is no lag time with their driving, so players must be quick about starting
    their vehicle when stopped, since there is slight a acceleration lag. Players who play the game enough notice
    incoming cars faster and can then hit the brake faster.

  • Prototype Assets:
    The full list of borrowed and modified assets include:
    • Car Model
    • Grass Tile
    • Road Tile
    • Explosion Animation
    • Accelerate/Brake Buttons
    • Flora
    • Medals
    • All audio assets

  • Prototype Closest Other Game:
    • Updates in this version include:
    • Fixed bugs with computer controlled opponent cars. Cars are less prone to stopping in the middle of the intersection.
    • Polished environment by adding trees and bushes to the roads.
    • Incoming cars now come in a number of colors to add variety to the game.
    • Opponents now have a stopping point and game displays medals to indicate who came in what position.
    • Players now have the option to choose the color of their cars. Opponent cars change to be different than the players.
    • Players additionally can choose the number of laps to run, as well as choose an "endless" mode.
