The Good, the Bad, and the Asteroid

  • High Concept:
    With the aid of very large man-made horseshoes, players battle an oncoming onslaught of
    asteroids and meteors headed towards Earth.

  • Theme:
    • Cowboys
    • Asteroid heading towards Earth

  • Mandated Variety:
    The genre of the game is sci-fi arcade, with no objectives in the game aside from
    accumulating a higher score with each playthrough. The asteroids and meteors spawn at
    random, therefore there is no fixed technique to the game and players must rely on reflexes.

  • Player Experience Goals:
    • Simple arcade gameplay; the game tests player reflexes instead of strategy or logic in
      a simple arcade-like format.
    • Acculumating score points; the ultimate goal of the player is to score as high as they
      can before losing the game.
    • Given the format of the game and the types of elements used (horseshoes), a small
      comedic effort was made to entertain or potentially humor the player.

  • Gameplay:
    Players use a point-and-click interface using the mouse pointer to fling horseshoes in the
    direction of the asteroids. Players gains +5 points for every asteroid hit, additionally,
    players start off with 3 health points and lose a health point each time they are hit by
    an asteroid.

  • Prototype Goal:
    This prototype is a beginners effort at Phaser and Javascript, and the mechanics tested
    include:
    • Spawning sprites at random locations on the borders of the game window.
    • Collectively directing sprites towards a specific point in the game.
    • Collision and overlay mechanics.
    • Shooting in the direction of a pointer; taking firing rate into account.

  • Features:
    The initial premise of the game was designed in slightly B-rated movie/parody format.
    No main character or other characters exist in the game, the POV is that of the Earth
    in space; collectively combatting the asteroids. There is not much unique about the game
    aside from the concept and the execution.

  • Setting/Premise:
    An additional feature that was to be added to the game was a parody scene explaining how
    the human world has collapsed; with society returning to the days of the Old West. Sensing
    the coming asteroid apocalypse, humans fashioned a large sling to fling equally large
    horseshoes into space and defend themselves from the asteroid attack.

  • Story:
    This game does not have a story and instead focuses on the arcade gameplay mechanics.

  • Target Audience:
    This game is appropriote for all ages, and does not have a specific demographic in mind.

  • Play Time:
    The length of the game depends on how long the player can outlast the asteroids, and therefore
    can range from a few seconds to potentially 10 minutes or more.

  • Strategies:
    The game style and mechanics are arcade with emphesis on player hand-eye coordination. Little
    strategy is needed for the game; especially with enemies spawning at random.
